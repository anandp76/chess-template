May Trophy Bonanza!

May Trophy Bonanza!

Let's welcome Spring with a competitive online chess tournament. We will be using the Lichess.org platform.
National Master Abby Marshall will be providing commentary again on our Youtube Channel.

**NEW** We are now requiring all players to be on a zoom call to be eligible for any prizes

Special for this tournament: Trophy prizes! You asked and we listened. Maximum of one trophy per player. If there's a tie for a placement, tie breaks will be used to determine trophy prizes

K-3: Top 3 players receive a trophy along with a trophy for top Kindergartener, 1st Grader, 2nd Grader and 3rd grader

K-6: Top 3 players receive a trophy. Top 4th, 5th and 6th grader will also receive a trophy if they are not in the top 3 places

K-9: Top 3 players receive a trophy. Top 7th, 8th and 9th graders will also receive a trophy if they are not in the top 3 places

Please note: We have a minimum of 10 players required per section. If we do not reach the minimum, we will combine two sections.

Learn step-by-step

  • All Sections: 4 rounds of G/30;+5. Rounds will be paired after every game is completed from the previous round. We give a 5-10 minute grace period from the time the last game finishes. If you opt into our text message alerts, you will be alerted to when each round is beginning.
  • Pairings based on a player's lichess classic rating (not US Chess rating).
  • Pairings are done automatically by the lichess system and cannot be changed.
  • Tournament prizes awarded up to 2 weeks after the tournament to allow for games to be screened for fair play. Registration Details:
  • Open to all players, K-9.
  • Players will receive an email with a link and password to join the appropriate section of the tournament.
  • Players must have a lichess account (if you do not have one, you must create one https://lichess.org/signup).
  • Players join the Chess Katz team in lichess https://lichess.org/team/chesskatz-tournaments after registering.
  • Registration closes at 11:00 AM EST the day of each tournament
  • BYES POLICY Online registration closes the day of the event at 11:00 AM EST. Players may request a 1/2 bye in round one (players who "late join" after 12:00 PM will also receive a 1/2 bye). No other 1/2 byes can be requested.
